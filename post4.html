Password Managers 101

Tired of remembering dozens of passwords? A password manager helps you store and manage your login credentials securely and conveniently.

What Is a Password Manager?

Itâ€™s a tool that creates, stores, and autofills your passwords. All your credentials are encrypted and stored in one secure vault, and you only need to remember one master password.

Why Use One?

Password managers generate strong, unique passwords for each account. They reduce the risk of reusing passwords and falling victim to data breaches.

Popular Options

  • 1Password
  • LastPass
  • Bitwarden (open-source and free)
  • Dashlane

Tip:

Always enable two-factor authentication on your password manager for extra security.
