Top 5 Cyber Hygiene Tips

Practicing good cyber hygiene is like brushing your teeth—simple daily habits that protect you in the long run. In this post, we explore 5 essential tips that will help you stay safe online.

1. Use Strong, Unique Passwords

Avoid reusing passwords across different sites. Use a mix of upper/lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Consider using a password manager to keep track.

2. Keep Software Up to Date

Updates often contain security patches. Enable automatic updates for your OS, browsers, antivirus, and apps.

3. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

This adds a second layer of protection. Even if someone gets your password, 2FA can stop them from accessing your account.

4. Be Wary of Phishing Emails

Don’t click on suspicious links or download unexpected attachments. Verify the sender’s address and be cautious of urgent-sounding messages.

5. Use Antivirus and a Firewall

A good antivirus can detect and remove threats. Firewalls act as a barrier between your device and unwanted traffic from the internet.
